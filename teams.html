SMbld

Competing since 2009, SMbld is the founding FRC team of Santa Margarita Catholic High School's robotics program. With over 15 seasons of experience, the team earned the prestigious Rookie All Star Award in their very first year and has been a consistent competitor in the FIRST California District.

SMbly Required

Since their rookie year in 2016, SMbly Required has rapidly risen in the FRC community. Currently ranked #47 in the California District.
